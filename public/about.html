About Mondai

Mondai is the best way to discover your campus and get your questions answered by individuals around you.


Know your campus. Locally.

The goal of Mondai is to help you get the most out of your college experience by learning from those who have been through it already. We are here to help connect you and your campus together.

There's more to college life then classes.

We believe that everyone knows some secret about their college, whether it be what class to take for a particular major or the best place to hang out on the weekends. During college, most of us don't have resources that allow us to ask these specific questions and get the best answers. Mondai was born as an idea to connect you to your fellow college mates who share the same interests or have experienced something that you want to experience.

Let Mondai bring more to your college life.
