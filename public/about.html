About Mondai

Mondai is the best way to discover your campus and get your questions answered by the people around you.


Know your campus. Locally.

The goal of Mondai is to help you get the most of your college life and enjoy the best experience from the people that shared the same experiences with you. We're here to help connect you and your campus together.

There's more to college life then classes.

We believe that everyone knows some secret about their college, whether it is the easy class for a particular major or what is the best place to hang out for History majors. When we're going through college, we don't have the tools that would allow us to ask these questions and get the coolest answer. Mondai was born as an idea to connect you to your fellow college mates that share the same interest or had an experience before.

Let Mondai bring more to your college life.
